About the program

Williamson County mountain biking

Wilco MTB is a NICA program serving student-athletes across Williamson County. The teams train together under one program and race as two squads — Wilco Composite and Independence. placeholder copy — replace with your story

Who we serve

Middle and high school riders (grades 6–12). Independence is for athletes zoned to Independence High School; everyone else rides with WilCo Composite. All skill levels welcome.

What is NICA?

The National Interscholastic Cycling Association runs interscholastic mountain bike racing nationwide. We compete in the Tennessee League across a fall race series.

The season

Preseason practices begin in April, building skills and fitness through the summer, leading into the NICA Tennessee race rounds in late summer and fall.

Safety first

All coaches are NICA-certified and background-checked. Helmets required, ride groups are sized by ability, and we follow NICA's coaching standards at every practice.

Questions, answered

Does my rider need a mountain bike?

A working mountain bike with front suspension and a helmet is the baseline. We can point you to local shops and loaner options to get started. placeholder

What does it cost?

Three parts per rider: NICA membership ($50 + $5 tech fee), Tennessee League membership ($100, plus $20–$30 per race), and team fees ($125). NICA and League fees are paid in PitZone; team fees go to @wilcocompmtb via Venmo by July 1. Full breakdown is on the Join tab.

How often are practices?

Multiple practices per week at rotating trails around the region. The full calendar lives on the Races tab and syncs from the team app.

Do I need experience?

No. Riders are grouped by ability and coached from wherever they're starting. Many join having never raced.

What it costs

Season fees

Three pieces make up a rider's season. NICA and Tennessee League fees are paid in PitZone; team fees are paid to the team via Venmo.

$50 / student-athlete

Plus a $5 technology fee per registrant. Registered coaches are $25.

Paid in PitZone

$100 / student-athlete

Plus race fees: $20 per middle school race, $30 per high school race. Register all at once or race by race.

Due via Venmo to @wilcocompmtb by July 1, before any participation. Covers the team tent, supplies, recovery zone, end-of-year party, and coach appreciation.
