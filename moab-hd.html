About the Series

Sponsored by MOAB Bike Shop, the Hammer Drop MTB Series is a grassroots, fun, competitive mountain bike race series featuring short-track, endurance (cross-country), and time-trial/technical trail racing across Middle Tennessee. One summer. Three race formats. Endless opportunities to throw down. A series designed to grow the local MTB racing community.

How Points Eligibility Works
01
Choose your level at registration
Register on BikeReg and select your skill level. Your category determines which race (20 min or 30 min) you are points-eligible in each Short Track night. All categories at Henry Horton and Barfield are automatically points-eligible.
02
One points race per night
You can only earn series points in one race per Short Track event night. If you ride both races, only your registered category race counts for points. The other race is Not Points Eligible (NPE) — results shown but not scored.
03
Cross-racing for training is welcome
Want extra miles? Enter both races on a given night. Only your category-eligible race counts for points. The other race is listed as NPE in results. Miles still count toward your ride total.
04
Race director may adjust your category
At their discretion, the race director may ask a rider to move from the 20 min points race to the 30 min points race to keep competition appropriate and fair for everyone on the course.
RD decisions are final and made in the interest of fair competition for all riders.

How progression is measured
A proprietary performance improvement score calculated across the full series — not a single race result
01
Tracks your trajectory
Results are charted race over race to measure how your performance trend lines move throughout the full season.
02
Relative to the field
Improvement is measured against the rest of the field — not just your own times — so race conditions and competition strength are factored in.
03
Every race counts
The algorithm looks at your complete body of work across all Hammer Drop events — no single standout result wins this award.
The spirit of this award
The Wahoo Fitness Progression Award exists to celebrate growth over glory. You don't need to win a race. You don't need to podium. You just need to show up, race hard, and get better — and the data will tell the story.
